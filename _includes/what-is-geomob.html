What is #geomob?

Started in 2008 in London, #geomob is a series of regular events in various European cities for location based service creators. We meet every few months, and provide a relaxed forum to discuss geoinnovation - whether for fun or profit.
Please get in touch if you would like to speak, or take a look at the directory of past speakers.

Become a #geomob sponsor

#geomob is made possible by our generous hosts and sponsors. If your brand would like to help foster geoinnovation in London please get in touch.

Sponsoring #geomob is a very low-effort, highly affordable way to reach the London location based service developer community. Sponsorship costs either £150 for a single event or £600 for an annual package (6 events).

Sponsors receive:

  • logo on our website
  • branding at the event in the form of a logo in the intro presentation
  • a mention on our twitter account before each event
  • a dedicated post on the site at the start of the sponsorship (annual package only)
  • 2 minutes to address the audience at the start of each event. Typically we have 50-75 attendees.
  • good geokarma
  • we welcome all ideas for deeper partnership that fit the #geomob ethos. A great example is the audience voted SplashMaps prize for best speaker.
The sponsorship funds are used exclusively to pay for the post-event pub visits where attendees discuss the ideas and start-ups presented. If this is of interest to your brand please email geomob @ opencagedata.com. We look forward to hearing from you. Many thanks to all past and present sponsors.
